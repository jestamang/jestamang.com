Privacy Policy

The Mythology of Your Data
I. What the Circus Collects

When you join the inner circle of Jestamang, we collect what you offer freely: your email address, a chosen username, and the details of your natal dossier — birthdate, birthplace, time of arrival into this peculiar world. We collect no more than what the ritual requires.

When you visit this site, the usual spectral traces are left — browser type, device, general geographic region — gathered not by us but by the infrastructure that carries these transmissions across the void (GitHub Pages, Firebase). We do not sell, trade, or auction off this information to anyone.

II. Cookies and Their Purpose

This site uses cookies solely to maintain your login session through Firebase Authentication. These are functional cookies — without them, the door to your dossier would not open. No tracking cookies. No advertising pixels. No surveillance apparatus of any kind. Simply the key to your own vault.

III. How Your Data Is Used

Your information is used exclusively to operate the features of this site: authentication, displaying your profile, saving your celestial chart, and storing transmissions you choose to save. We use Firebase (a Google service) to handle authentication and data storage; their privacy practices apply to the infrastructure layer.

Your natal chart data and any transmissions you post to the blog are stored on our servers. What you share publicly is public. What you keep private stays in your dossier alone.

IV. Your Rights Over Your Data

You may delete your account at any time from your profile page. Upon deletion, your authentication record and Firestore data are removed from the system. Some residual data may persist in server logs for a brief period before natural expiration.

If you wish to request a copy of your data or ask any questions regarding this policy, you may transmit your inquiry through the Email page.

V. Third-Party Services

This site integrates with Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage, and EmailJS for contact form delivery. These services have their own privacy policies which govern their handling of data. Links to their respective platforms are maintained separately.

VI. Changes to This Policy

Should the mythology evolve, this page will be updated to reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance. We will not make changes that diminish your rights without notice.

VII. Contact

For questions, corrections, or transmissions regarding this policy, reach us at the Email portal or directly at thejestamang@gmail.com.
