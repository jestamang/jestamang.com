Let's Roll

The bones speak. Seven liberates. Seven destroys. The table remembers everything.

Come Out Roll
CHIPS: 500   BET: 0
Choose Pass Line or Don't Pass, place your bet, then roll.
  • Select a chip and click the Pass or Don't Pass bet area to wager
  • Come-out roll: rolling 7 or 11 wins Pass bets; rolling 2, 3, or 12 loses them
  • Any other number becomes the Point — roll the Point again before rolling a 7 to win Pass
  • Rolling a 7 before hitting the Point wins Don't Pass bets
  • Keep betting rounds to grow or protect your stack
