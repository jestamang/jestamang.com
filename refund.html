Refund Policy

The Terms of Exchange
I. The Nature of the Transaction

Every artifact that leaves the Jestamang vault and arrives at your door carries with it the spirit of the work. We take these exchanges seriously — because the things we make are not mass-produced objects but genuine transmissions from the collective. For this reason, our refund policy is built on fairness rather than fine print.

II. Physical Goods — Merch and Artifacts

If a physical item arrives damaged, defective, or fundamentally different from what was described, we will make it right. Contact us within 14 days of receiving your order through the Email portal with a brief description and photograph of the issue. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you.

If you simply change your mind after an order has shipped, we are unable to offer a refund — the artifact has already begun its journey. However, if the item has not yet shipped, reach out promptly and we will cancel the order without question.

III. Digital Goods

Digital downloads and digital access are non-refundable once delivered, as the transmission cannot be un-received. If you believe you were charged in error or encountered a technical failure that prevented delivery, contact us and we will investigate with an open hand.

IV. Membership and Inner Circle Access

Membership in the inner circle is free. There is nothing to refund. If you wish to leave, you may delete your account from your profile page at any time, and all your data will be removed from the system.

V. How to Reach Us

All refund requests, order inquiries, and related transmissions should be sent through the Email page or directly to thejestamang@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 3 business days. The circus is small but attentive.

VI. Final Note

We are not a faceless operation. Every inquiry reaches a real person who cares about the work and about you. If something went wrong, tell us — we would rather fix it than lose your trust.
